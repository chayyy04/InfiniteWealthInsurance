Frequently Asked Questions

Is AIA Critical Protect 100 different from HMO? +
Yes! HMO plans are annual and don't build cash value. AIA Critical Protect 100 is a lifetime insurance plan that pays you a lump sum cash benefit upon diagnosis of a critical illness — which you can use however you need, not just for hospital bills.
Can I apply even if I have a pre-existing condition? +
It depends on the condition. Our life planners will walk you through the underwriting process honestly. Some conditions may result in exclusions, while others may still qualify for coverage. Book a free consultation and we'll assess your situation personally.
How long until I can claim benefits? +
A waiting period typically applies for critical illness claims (usually 90 days from policy issuance). Once the waiting period is complete and a qualifying diagnosis is made, claims are processed promptly. AIA Philippines has a strong track record of honoring claims.
What happens if I stop paying premiums? +
AIA plans typically have a grace period. If premiums lapse, the policy may use accumulated cash value (for applicable plans) or become reduced paid-up. Our team will explain all options to make sure your coverage stays intact.
